Custom actions


Custom actions are buttons that perform specific tasks on specific items that would normally be performed from the lower panel on one host at a time, such as startting or stopping a specific application.
By creating a custom action button commonly needed actions can be accomplished on one or more machines simultaneously without needing to first select the appropriate group tab and item needed. Some actions can also be performed on multiple items at once.
Custom action buttons can be added to the standard action buttons on the left side of the top panel using the Add custom action button:
After a custom action button has been created it can be renamed or removed by right clicking on the button.
